Bio

I am a graduate student in Statistics and Operations Research at the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill (UNC).

My current research interests are in both deterministic and stochastic optimisation.

I enjoy rock-climbing, sporting clays, rifle & pistol shooting, cycling (in cool weather), strategy board games, hiking, collecting luxury pens, and craft beer.
